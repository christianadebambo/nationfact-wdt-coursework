About NationFact

With the global lifting of Covid-19 lockdown restrictions, international travel has once again found its footing. This prompted us to create NationFact, a website that will enable tourists and other travelers plan their travels by viewing background information of countries, locating capital cities around the world and comparing the cost of living in these capitals.
